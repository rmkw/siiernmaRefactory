Objetivos de Desarrollo Sostenible. ODS.

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to end poverty, protect the planet and ensure that by 2030 all people will enjoy peace and prosperity.

The SDGs are designed to end poverty, hunger, AIDS and discrimination against women and girls.

The creativity, knowledge, technology and financial resources of the whole society are necessary to achieve the SDGs in all contexts.
